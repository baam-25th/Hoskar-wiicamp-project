What is
HoSkar Night ?

A new way of connecting people and ideas, HoSkar Night is the largest hybrid networking event for hospitality and real estate senior professionals in Vietnam.

Meet people, be seen, be heard, learn, and most of all, have fun!

HoSkar Night also offers an amazing Business to Business channel for brands to gain more exposure in hospitality and real estate industry.
